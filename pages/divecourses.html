    Discovery of Scuba Diving

    This one-day PADI course gives you the opportunity to scuba dive to a depth of 12 meters. Although it doesn't lead to a certification, it can count as your first open water dive for the purposes of earning your PADI open water certification. People who want to learn what it's like to breathe underwater and experience the undersea world should definitely check out this course.

    Price

    575 AED

    Four training days : Five Knowledge Development theory workshops. Five pool dives to develop fundamental scuba abilities. Four open water dives are available for you to test your abilities and explore, including four dives on each beach.

    Master Scuba Diver

    The PADI Program of Diver training has a Master Scuba Diver rating, which is the highest non-professional level. To earn this level of certification, you must record a specified number of dives and be proficient in at least five scuba diving-specific fields.
