Dive Sites to visit

There are many exciting dive sites for you to explore.
Check this information and be ready to immerse yourself in the beauty of these countries.

Maldives

Reef, North Male Atoll
One of the Maldives' oldest reefs and it is ornamented with caverns, towering cliffs, lovely corals, and strong overhangs. Sharks, barracudas, and groupers can be seen.

    Egypt

    Ricks Reef, Red Sea
    where all levels of divers may do this popular and simple drift dive. a tiny crack in the reef table and a sloping area covered in salad corals.

    Philippines

    Cathedral, Batangas
    Soft reefs and corals sponges, nudibranchs, and tropical species, including angelfish, butterfly fish and clownfish, may be found.

    Thailand

    Stonehenge, Kohlipe
    Stonehenge's a wonderfully colorful reef of soft corals in shallow water. There's explosively colored soft coral covering these rocks..

    Hawaii

    Manta Ray, Kailua Kona
    Beautiful natural creatures, including Manta rays, can be seen while diving at night near a lit-up raft.

    Australia

    Ningaloo Reef,
    Plenty of rocky ledges and coral gardens. Sites for humpback whales, dolphins, sea snakes, turtles, manta rays, and ospreys..
